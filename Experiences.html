Building Various Machine Learning Models To Identify Used Vehicle Prices

Working closely with Raytheon Technologies and Jarick Cammarato, models such as Artificial Neural Networks, Support Vector Machines, K-Nearest Neighbor Algorithms, Decision Trees/Random Forest, and much more were built and their relative accuracies were determined.

It took roughly 250 hours to accomplish this project that required significant documentation and entailed a year's worth of work. We concluded by showcasing our respective work. My specific project was published as an open source project and the website was deployed for all people to use.

The mean absolute percent error (MAPE) between predicted and actual prices was calculated for each algorithm. An ensemble of the models with a significantly high predictive accuracy was created and their hyperparameters were optimized. Finally, a website employing HTML, CSS, and PHP (Table 1) was created. The complete model was then uploaded to the site for public usage (Figure 1)."

"The project's objective was met. An ensemble model greatly outperformed an individual ANN model. A robust model with a mean absolute percent error of 5.86% was constructed by integrating an ANN and an RF model using simple averaging."

Molecular Modeling of Beta 2 Adrenergic Receptors to examine polymorphisms impact on protein functionality

1 in 12 people, around 8% of the population, have asthma and those numbers are growing every year (CDC, 2009). Asthma attacks can be highly detrimental, and even fatal. The severity and frequency of Asthma attacks can be traced back to specific amino acid swaps on chromosome 5, which encodes for the Beta 2 Adrenergic Receptor (B2AR). Adrenergic Receptors cover a wide range of functions, including wakefulness, cognition, and mood. B2AR’s are specifically found in the airway smooth muscle and regulate contractions and relaxations. B2AR’s are G-protein receptors, which are activated by agonists such as Epinephrine.

 Once a ligand has bonded to the receptors, the GDP on the alpha unit converts into a GTP, causing the alpha, beta, and gamma subunits detach from the receptor and activate Adenylate Cyclase which converts ATP into cAMP, amplifying the signal. Specific to B2AR, the cAMP proceeds activate Protein Kinase A which activates a chain of enzymes to cause smooth muscle relaxation (Johnson, 2006). However, certain amino acid swaps lead to polymorphisms in the proteins function. For example, the Glycine/Arginine Swap at location 16 allow for desensitization of the receptor worsening asthma, while a glutamine/glutamate swap at location 27 prevents desensitization, and eases the effect of asthma (Johnson, 2006; Wendy et al., 1993). These complicated functions and polymorphisms of the Beta-2 Adrenergic receptors can only be shown by modeling the protein to fully understand how amino acid swap’s effect on the conformation of the protein affects the protein’s function. 

The project entailed detailed reasearch into the causes and effects of the B2AR protein on maintaining human systems. This was followed with fully modeling the structure of the protein, including the proteins primary, secondary, and tertiary stuctures. The structures and R-groups interactions were taking into consideration to form a model of the protein. And finally known mutations were examined and the reason for their effects on individuals were studied. The entire project lasted 4-5 months.

I nvestigating the Visual Cortex and Emotional States relation to Time Perception

We surveyed students around our school and examined how induced emotions affect the passage of time. These emotion included anger, sadness, joy, and fear. Minitab was used to run statistical tests including a 4 way Anova.

The results showed that their was a significance lengthening of perceived time when exposed to fearful stimuli. There was also a percived shortening of time when exposed to Joyful stimuli. This was then linked to your brains processing speed, which increases in fearful situation to keep you safe but relaxes in more calming and relaxing environments.
